**Life at the Crossroads: Weaver, Indiana**

A documentary outlining the history of Weaver, an early African-American community in Grant County.

Produced in 1999 by Matthew Voss and Aaron Sickler for the Community History Project.

*The original video documentary can be viewed on Vimeo: [vimeo.com/25634095](https://vimeo.com/25634095)*
